Signs of Deepfake Manipulation

While deepfakes can be convincing at first glance, there are often subtle signs that can help you identify them. Here's what to look for:

Visual Clues
Facial Features
  • Unnatural eye shape, color, or reflections
  • Blurry or poorly defined facial features
  • Inconsistent skin textures or unnatural smoothness
  • Strange or missing teeth
Boundaries and Edges
  • Blurry or distorted boundaries between face and hair
  • Unnatural transitions between face and neck
  • Missing or oddly shaped earlobes
Technical Artifacts
Common Deepfake Errors
  • Inconsistent lighting across the face
  • Unnatural or missing shadows
  • Flickering or warping at the edges
  • Strange color bleeding or artifacts
Metadata Clues
  • Missing or inconsistent EXIF data
  • Unusual compression artifacts
  • Evidence of editing in image analysis
Pro Tip

When in doubt, look at the background for inconsistencies. Deepfake algorithms often focus on the face and may neglect proper integration with the background.
